Simple Omelet Recipe

An easy and quick dish perfect for any meal.The classic meal combines beaten eggs cooked for perfection, optionally filled with your choice of cheese, vegetables or meats.

preparation time

  • Total: Approximately 10 minutes
  • preparation time : 5 minutes
  • cooking time:5 minutes

Ingridients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • salt to taste
  • pepper to taste
  • 1 table spoon of butter or oil
  • optional fillings:cheese, diced vegetables, cooked meats, herbs

Instructions

  1. Beat the eggs: In a bowl, beat the eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper until they are well mixed. You can add a tablespoon of water or milk for a fluffer texture
  2. Heat the pan:place a non stick frying pan over medium heat and add butter or oil.
  3. cook the omelete:once the butter melted and bubbling,pour in the eggs. Tilt the pan to enure the egg evenly coat the surface.
  4. Add fillings(optional):When the egg begins to set in the edges but are slightly runny in the middle sprinkle your choosen fillings over one half of THE omelete
  5. Fold and serve:As the omelete continues to cook, carefully lift one edge and fold it over the fillings.Let it cook for anonter minute, and slide it into a plate.
  6. Enjoy:Serve hot with additional salt and pepper if needed.
